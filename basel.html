Basel University

It is one of the oldest universities in Switzerland and is located in the city of Basel, where it was inaugurated on April 4, 1460, and has witnessed many developments since its establishment, and during the first year after its establishment, the university registry record enrolled 226 students, and today the university contains seven colleges It includes about 13,000 students and more than 350 professors.


Basel admission requirements

The application process for university enrollment is made for all three levels of bachelors, masters, and doctoral degrees. It is done online, and there are separate procedures for enrollment in both the Faculty of Medicine and Dentistry. In the case of seeking admission to postgraduate studies in the field of dentistry, a request for a joint master’s program must be submitted In computational biology and bioinformatics, the deadline for application is April 30 in the first semester, and for the second semester on October 15, students can apply for several courses.

Kingdom students can apply to join the undergraduate programs at the deadline of April 30 in the first semester, and for the second semester on October 15, complete the application form and attach some documents for the student, namely:

- A high school diploma translated and accredited.
- recommendation Letter .
-Certificate of English language proficiency.
- passport copy.
