Paulista University

Established in 1988, Universidade Paulista (Paulista University) is a for-profit private higher education institution located in the metropolis of São Paulo (population range of over 5,000,000 inhabitants), Sao Paulo. Officially accredited and/or recognized by the Ministério da Educação, Brazil (Ministry of Education, Brazil), Universidade Paulista (UNIP) is a very large (uniRank enrollment range: over-45,000 students) coeducational higher education institution. Universidade Paulista (UNIP) offers courses and programs leading to officially recognized higher education degrees such as bachelor degrees in several areas of study. See the uniRank degree levels and areas of study matrix below for further details. This 32 years old higher-education institution has a selective admission policy based on entrance examinations.


Paulista admission requirements

The student must choose the institute or university and download the application form. After this is completed, it should be sent to the institute along with previous education certificates.
English language tests should also be included if applicable.
After that, the educational institution reviews the application and decides whether or not to accept it. In the event of acceptance, the student is notified, with an acceptance letter from the institution.

Accepted Test

Students are required to take the admission exam before enrolling, each university has its own exam. Depending on the institution of higher education, you are required to appear as either a Vestibular Vestibular Exam or an INEM exam, or both.
