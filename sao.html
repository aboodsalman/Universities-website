Sao Paulo University

Federal government university is considered one of the most important universities for higher education, research and experiences in all fields of knowledge. It is considered one of the largest public universities in Brazil and the third largest university in all Latin America, the sixth largest university in Brazil in terms of students, and ranks first as the best university in Latin America and the ninety-fourth among all universities in the world. It is managed by the state government of San Paolo.

The university is divided into 8 fields (Campi) in the capital Sao Paulo, Main Building, Bauru, Lorena, Piracicaba, Piracunonga, Ribeirao Preto, and two fields in São Carlos.

Among public universities, they provide the largest number of seats in undergraduate and graduate studies in Brazil, and are also responsible for graduating the largest number of professors and doctoral degrees in the world, representing half of the total scientific production of the state of San Paulo and more than 25% of Brazil.


Sao Paulo admission requirements

In order to obtain admission to higher education institutions, students must complete a secondary education level and pass a competitive examination to determine if a student is eligible to enter a specific institution. A decade ago, the Brazilian government launched the ENEM (Secondary Education Assessment Exam). Since then, some higher education institutions have accepted admission as part of the admission process.

The common elements for admission to Brazilian universities are as follows:

-The student must choose the institute or university and download the application form. After this is completed, it should be sent to the institute along with previous education certificates.
-English language tests should also be included if applicable.
-After that, the educational institution reviews the application and decides whether or not to accept it. In the event of acceptance, the student is notified, with an acceptance letter from the institution that students must apply for the student card or visa. accepted test

Students are required to take the admission exam before enrolling, each university has its own exam. Depending on the higher education institution, you are required to appear as either a Vestibular Vestibular Exam or an INEM exam, or both.
